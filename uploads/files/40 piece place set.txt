<div class="product-info">
<div class="entry-content">
<p>Set a luxurious--yet surprisingly affordable--table with Ricci's Cellini 40-piece flatware set. The design incorporates long, slightly flared handles with an acanthus leaf decoration at each tip and neck. An inset thread border runs along the squared edges for added dimension, and the mirror finish catches the light with a sparkling gleam. Always committed to high-quality materials and workmanship, Ricci continues the decoration on both sides of the handle for an elegant and seamless presentation.</p>
<p>Crafted of 18/10 stainless steel--with 18 percent chrome for corrosion-resistance and 10 percent nickel for a silver-like luster--the flatware exhibits superior balance and feels pleasantly weighty in the hand. All pieces go safely into the dishwasher and will maintain their radiance for years to come without polishing. </p>
<strong>About Ricci Argentieri Flatware</strong>
<p>One hundred and sixty years ago the Ricci family artisans established their Guild House, Ricci Argentieri, in Alesandria, a small northern Italy town near Milan. Since, 1840, Ricco Argentieri (also known as Ricci Silversmith) has been one of the most respected and renowned silversmiths in all of Europe. Ricci's rich tradition of quality craftsmanship continues today in the USA.</p>
